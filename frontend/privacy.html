3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

4. Data Storage and Security

Your OAuth credentials are stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:

  • Temporary Access Keys: When sharing credentials with Bitcast Network nodes, we use time-limited access keys that automatically expire after one hour
  • Controlled Access: Only authorized nodes within the Bitcast Network can receive access keys
  • Secure Storage: Your primary OAuth credentials remain securely stored on our protected servers

5. Data Sharing

We share your OAuth credentials in a limited and controlled manner specifically for verification of video performance on behalf of sponsors:

  • Bitcast Network Nodes: Your YouTube OAuth credentials are shared with authorized verification nodes within the Bitcast Network for video performance analysis
  • Temporary Access: Sharing is accomplished through temporary access keys that expire after one hour
  • Limited Purpose: This sharing is solely for the purpose of enabling video performance verification and accessing your YouTube data as authorized

We do not:

  • Sell or trade your information for commercial purposes
  • Share your data with parties outside the Bitcast Network ecosystem
  • Provide permanent or unrestricted access to your credentials

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke authorization at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of your stored credentials by contacting us
  • Access: You can request information about what data we have stored for you

7. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with:

  • Google/YouTube: We use Google's OAuth 2.0 service to access YouTube data. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.

8. Data Retention
